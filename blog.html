The King Of Asia

Al Hilal Saudi Football Club (Arabic: نادي الهلال السعودي), simply known as Al Hilal is a professional multi-sports club based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Their football team competes in the Saudi Professional League. In Arabic, Al Hilal means the crescent moon. They are the most decorated club in Asia, winning 68 official trophies. Al Hilal also hold the record for the most continental trophies in Asia, as well as a record 19 Professional League titles.[3] Founded on 16 October 1957, Al Hilal are one of three teams to have participated in all seasons of the Saudi Professional League since its establishment in 1976.

Guinness World Record

The most consecutive men's association football victories across all competitions is 34, and was achieved by Al Hilal (Saudi Arabia) of the Saudi Pro League between 25 September 2023 and 11 April 2024. Al Hilal compiled their record winning streak across four competitions – the Saudi Pro League, the King Cup, Saudi Super Cup and the AFC Champions League. The streak finally came to an end on 17 April 2024, when Al Hilal lost 4–2 away to Al Ain in the first leg of the AFC Champions League semi-finals.
